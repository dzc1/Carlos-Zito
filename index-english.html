Our Top Requested Treatments

Aesthetic beauty is based on proportions & volume.

BOTOX

One of the most effective treatments within Aesthetic Medicine for facial rejuvenation. It is the only medicine approved to prevent or provisionally eliminate wrinkles on the forehead, frown lines between the eyebrows (glabellar lines) and, crow’s feet in a secure and simple way without having to go to surgery.

FILLERS

Discover all the possibilities that you can reach through the application of hyaluronic acid. This innovative technology helps hydrate and increase the volume of your skin giving you a younger and healthier look. Hyaluronic acid accentuates the natural features of your face like your cheeks and jaw.

Rhinoplasty without surgery

With the application of this treatment you will be able to improve and fix your nasal features like the elevation of the tip of the nose, your nasal angles and even get a slim and lean nose through this secure procedure without the bare of going under surgery and, a quick recovery. This procedure is used to fix some of the errors of previous nasal surgeries.

Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP)

​Platelet rich plasma consists of a concentrated preparation of the patient’s own platelets from centrifugation of blood and extraction of the separated platelet-rich layer resulting in the synthesis of new collagen and elastin cells.The result is a younger, radiant and healthier looking skin.

More Info

Designer Lips

Achieve that structured and hydrated form to your lips with our hyaluronic acid fillers. Giving back volume and even increasing its size. Get natural results through this fast and secure procedure and get the lips that you’ve always dreamed of.

More Info

Carboxytherapy

Carboxytherapy employs CO2 injections or transdermal application to infuse gaseous carbon dioxide below the skin into the subcutaneous tissue through a needle or skin. It promotes tissue oxygenation, collagen neoformation and lipolytic effects. Improving stretch marks, acne marks, flaccidity, hypertrophic and post-surgical scars.
